Food Description
Chicken Tenders Whether it's grilled or fried, you have got to try our chicken tenders! Expertly seasoned batter makes these a delight for even the pickiest eaters. Try them plain, dipped in a variety of sauces, or covered in buffalo sauce for a spicy kick.
All American Cheeseburgers Our all-beef burgers are cooked to order and come with american cheese, grilled onions, lettuce, and pickles by default. But don't stop there! Feel free to add any of our other toppings, including bacon, mushrooms, and more!
Grilled Hotdogs These dogs will make you bark! Whether you want beef or pork, we have it here at Showtime. Dress it out any way you like. Don't forget the fries!
Speciality Wings You only thought you had good wings before you came to Showtime Gamebar. Try them grilled or fried and hand-tossed in any of our sauces, including lemon pepper, teriyaki, and spicy lava. Can you handle the goodness?
Sandwiches All the classics are here! Whether you're in the mood for a hoagie or just a plain old grilled cheese sandwich, we've got you covered!
Sliders Pulled pork? Check. Fried chicken breast? Yum. If you can't decide on a burger, the possibilities are endless here. Mix and match our sliders for a flavor fest!
